About FreshBite

Where Taste Meets Innovation — Crafting unforgettable food journeys.

Our Story

FreshBite began with a simple dream — to bring people together through food. Founded by culinary enthusiasts, our mission is to create bold, unforgettable flavors while staying rooted in freshness, sustainability, and creativity. From humble pop-up beginnings to becoming a city-favorite destination, we’ve remained true to our purpose: To produce food of the highest standard.

About Fresh Bite

Fresh Bite is a major supplier of sandwiches, baguettes, ciabatta, focaccia, curries, salads, pasta, and noodles within the food industry.

We currently supply universities, colleges, hospitals, and over 1,000 independent retail stores, including petrol stations. We manufacture and distribute our fresh products six days a week.

We love visiting companies for food tasting appointments, so you and your team can fully appreciate the quality of our products.

We offer competitive discounts, as well as a sale-or-return option on our products.

OUR SUPPLIERS

We work closely with our British farmers and suppliers to produce food of the highest standard using freshly prepared ingredients.

Our meat is prepared using Halal Methods and just like our meat, our vegetables and fruit are also from the UK.

Meet the talented team who make all this happen

Binda Sangha

Managing Director

With over 30 years of experience in the UK food industry, Binda Sangha brings deep industry knowledge and a proven track record of excellence in both food production and supply chain management. Having worked across a wide range of sectors—including wholesale, distribution, and retail—he has developed an in-depth understanding of the operational demands and regulatory standards that define the food business today. From ensuring food safety compliance to streamlining logistics and procurement, Binda has led teams through significant growth and transformation while maintaining the highest standards of quality. Under his leadership, the company continues to build strong partnerships, implement efficient supply chain practices, and deliver reliable, high-quality food solutions to clients across the UK. Binda's passion for the industry and hands-on approach inspire our team to remain focused on excellence, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement.
